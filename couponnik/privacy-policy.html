Introduction

The “Get Code” coupons are those users get at a coupon site instead of a store where they want to make a purchase. Shoppers select the needed coupon, click the "Get code" button, and open a promo code. Then they go to the selected online retail store, add the desired products to the shopping cart, and enter the previously received promo code at the checkout to get a discount.
